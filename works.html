Cobbling my identity together in the imaginary maize field. Mycelium hums beneath the surface, threading unseen connections. Fermenting ecological and anti-capitalist food systems, solidarity, and collectivism. Wild yeast breathes into dough, an invisible choreography of life. The body is a microbiome; what, then, is happening to our macrobiome? The difference is this gulf between people, one that can never be completely filled.
